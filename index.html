Our Students At Risk!

In October, 8th graders from Mr. M's Digital Literacy class designed a digital addiciton survey after studying the ways that social media are addictive and harmful.

The survey was shared with all crews, and a quarter of our students responded. 7th and 8th graders analyzed the results with Excel 365 ( more limited than Office Excel. Download Office when you can!

We are now sharing our results, as promised, with this webpage. Some of us also made infographics with LucidPress.com. Our first infographic was about social media and teen mental health, analyzing a survey of students in Western Massachusetts.

In our survey and readings, we found that kids are more anxious, more depressed, and sometimes suicidal. This is not only because of the Pandemic's affects on them and their families, but also because of how they used social media to cope with quarantine last year. This stayed with them, particularly 8th graders.

This is an ongoing problem for all of us, so please read what we found and think about how you can help!
